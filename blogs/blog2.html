A Lost Friend

Consider yourself in a hypothetical position where you are so insecure about everything. Everything that revolves around you. You end up suspecting each and everything , you are so perturbed by everything, that your relationships with people start to strain. You go through mental trauma and depressive disorders, you are now nothing more than a dead body with no soul.

You are revived by a Friend, a Friend that comes as a blessing, but this blessing has its own cons.

What happens when you are attracted to something or someone so much, that even the thought of losing that thing or the person, takes all your happiness, rues your day and you start to think so much that you end up losing your mental peace and stability.
People call me crazy, ignorant, stupid, dumbo, and what not. But this one Friend, uf this friend is unexplainable. Sometimes I used to question myself every night, is this real? is this a dream? I was such a simpleton that I even prayed for my friend. oh and when we came close, I remember praying sujud ash-shukr I was so happy that I Glorify Allah in all his forms, "oh Allah i cannot thank you enough for what you've given me" . You might be thinking was I really this stupid, really? YES!

I didn't know anything about philosophy and sufism until this very friend of mine suggested me to read "the forty rules of love" by Elif Shafak. And this very book just changed my perspective. The pinch to this story is that this friend of mine wont understand philosophy.

"Change is the only constant in life" said by god knows who, but whoever said it, must've been very wise. Change is only thing that keeps us going, if we dont change, we dont survive as simple as that. Had I restricted my self to your company I would have never met my very own self.

Life has been living hell for me, the past month has been so erratic, so confusing that even words cannot desciribe what I feel. No one is going to be there for you, even when they say it a thousand times. In the end you have to learn to love yourself becuase you are going to live with yourself for the rest of your life. When people get new friends they forget their old companion and move on and its better to keep only one companion and that is ALLAH.

"I have lived on the lip of insanity, wanting to know reasons, knocking on a door. It opens. I've been knocking from the inside." How true is that? Let me tell you: IT'S PRETTY TRUE.

Now everythingin rubble, does anything bother me anymore? YES and NO.
